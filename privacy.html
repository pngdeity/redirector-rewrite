Privacy Policy

Redirector Rewrite does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

Data Storage

All redirect rules and extension preferences are stored locally in your browser using the chrome.storage API. If you enable Storage Sync, rules are synced across your devices via your browser's sync service (Chrome Sync or Firefox Sync). The extension developer has no access to this data.

Network Requests

The extension intercepts outbound network requests to apply user-defined URL redirects. It does not send any data to external servers. The declarativeNetRequest API processes all redirects natively within the browser.

Permissions

  • storage — Stores your redirect rules and preferences locally
  • declarativeNetRequest — Intercepts and redirects network requests based on your rules
  • webNavigation — Detects single-page application navigation for redirects
  • tabs — Opens and updates tabs for redirects
  • notifications — Shows a notification when a redirect occurs (if enabled)
